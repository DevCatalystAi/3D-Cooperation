Our Services

PET Collection

We collect PET bottles from various sources across Nigeria, ensuring efficient and responsible gathering.

Sorting

Our team meticulously sorts collected bottles to prepare them for recycling processes.

Supply to Recycling Plants

We supply sorted PET bottles to recycling plants, supporting the circular economy.
